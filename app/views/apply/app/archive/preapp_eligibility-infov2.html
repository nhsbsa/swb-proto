Before you start an application

At the moment you can only use this service if both of these apply:

  • your pension is your only income
  • you live on your own

If you can't apply online yet

You need to apply using a paper form called an HC1.

You can download an HC1 or get one from Jobcentres, GP practices, pharmacies and Citizens Advice centres.

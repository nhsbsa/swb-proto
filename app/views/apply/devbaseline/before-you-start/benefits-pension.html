Guarantee Credit

Guarantee Credit tops up your weekly income if it’s below £159.35.

Savings Credit

Savings Credit is a payment for people who have saved some money towards their retirement, for example a pension.

The most in Savings Credit you can get per week is £13.20.

If you're still unsure contact the Pension Service helpline.

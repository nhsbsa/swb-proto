You get help with NHS costs but can't apply online

Apply for help with NHS costs

Because your local council helps to pay for your care home, you're entitled to help but need to apply using a paper form.

Next steps

You need to apply using a paper form called an 'HC1 SC'.

You can download an HC1 SC or get one from Jobcentres, GP practices, pharmacies and Citizens Advice centres.

What you'll get

You get free:

  • NHS prescriptions
  • NHS dental treatment
  • sight tests
  • NHS wigs and fabric supports

You get money off:

  • new glasses or contact lenses
  • repairing or replacing your glasses or contact lenses
  • travel for NHS treatment

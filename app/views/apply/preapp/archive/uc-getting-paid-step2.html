Universal timeline example
  • Ben lost his job and makes a new claim for Universal Credit on 15 July.
  • He must wait seven days before his claim can start, this is called the 'waiting week'.
  • This makes his assessment date the 22 July. It means he will be paid on the 22nd of each month.
  • He needs to wait one 'assessment period' (that’s a calendar month) to 22 August.
  • He also needs to leave up to seven days for the money to reach his account.
  • He should expect his first payment of Universal Credit no later than 29 August.

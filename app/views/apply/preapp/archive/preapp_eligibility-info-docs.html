You could apply for help with NHS costs online

This is a new service and we're only inviting a limited number of people to apply online.

If you apply online you'll:

  • complete your application in 10 to 15 minutes
  • get instant confirmation that we've received your application
  • get a reference number that you can use if you have any queries

What you need

When you apply, we'll ask for information about:

  • money you get- things like pay, pensions or student loans (your income)
  • your housing costs- things like council tax and rent (your outgoings)
  • any savings or investments you have

If you have a partner, you'll need to tell us about their income and outgoings too.

Documents that could help

You may need your:

  • payslips or latest business accounts (if self employed)
  • student funding notices
  • latest statement of benefits or award notice
  • pension details
  • savings, investments and property details
  • rent or mortgage statements
  • latest council tax bill or council tax benefit letter

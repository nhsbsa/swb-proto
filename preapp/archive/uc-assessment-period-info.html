You aren't automatically entitled to help with NHS costs

Because you're waiting on the outcome of your first assessment period, you won't know if you're entitled to help with your NHS costs.

You'll get help if you earned:

  • £435 or less
  • £935 or less if you have a payment for either a child who lives with you or a 'limited capability for work'

What to do next

After your first assessment period, you'll find out if you're automatically entitled to help.

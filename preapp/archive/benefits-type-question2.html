Moving to Universal Credit

The DWP will let you know when it’s time to make a claim for Universal Credit.

If you’re already getting any of the benefits to be replaced by Universal Credit and your circumstances stay the same, you don’t have to do anything. To find out what help with NHS costs you're entitled to, it's important that you don't guess the answer.
